How Registering as a Donor Helps

💻
Indexed in Live Directory: Your profile is instantly added to our secure searchable directory. Hospitals and approved local requesters can match with you instantly in emergencies.
⚡
Real-time Alert Broadcast: When a request matching your blood group is registered in your city, you will receive an instant notification to accept or decline.
🛡️
Privacy-First Safe Connection: Your telephone number is kept entirely confidential and only displayed to verified hospital administrators or requesters if you accept their request.

How Blood Donors Are Useful

❤️
1 Donation = 3 Lives Saved: A single standard donation (approx. 350-450ml) is separated into red blood cells, plasma, and platelets, which can be given to three different patients.
🔬
No Artificial Replacement: Human blood cannot be manufactured or synthetically synthesized in a lab. Patient care depends entirely on the goodwill of donors like you.
⏱️
Constant Critical Demand: Every 2 seconds, someone in India / globally needs a blood transfusion for major surgeries, cancer therapies, childbirth, or trauma.

Donor Health & Eligibility Rules

⚖️
Body Weight: Must weigh at least 45 kg to ensure a safe extraction volume without side effects.
🎂
Age Bracket: Must be between 18 and 65 years old to register.
⏱️
3-Month Gap Cooldown: You must wait at least 90 days between whole blood donations to ensure your body fully restores iron reserves.
🩺
General Health: Must be in good overall health, free from infectious diseases, and not under strong chronic medications.
